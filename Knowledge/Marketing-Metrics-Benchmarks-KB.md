## AARRR Pirate Metrics Framework

### Acquisition

| Metric | Formula | Description | Target |
|--------|---------|-------------|--------|
| **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)** | (Marketing + Sales Spend) / New Customers | Cost to acquire one customer | Industry dependent |
| **Cost Per Lead (CPL)** | Marketing Spend / Leads Generated | Cost to generate one lead | Channel dependent |
| **Cost Per Click (CPC)** | Ad Spend / Clicks | Cost per ad click | €0.50-5.00 |
| **Cost Per Mille (CPM)** | (Ad Spend / Impressions) × 1000 | Cost per 1000 impressions | €5-50 |
| **Traffic Sources** | Visitors by source | Where users come from | Diversified |
| **Organic/Paid Ratio** | Organic Traffic / Total Traffic | Marketing sustainability | > 50% organic |

**Acquisition by Channel Benchmarks:**

| Channel | B2B SaaS CPL | B2B SaaS CAC | E-commerce CAC |
|---------|--------------|--------------|----------------|
| Organic Search | €20-50 | €200-400 | €15-40 |
| Paid Search | €50-150 | €300-600 | €30-80 |
| Social Organic | €30-70 | €250-500 | €20-50 |
| Social Paid | €40-100 | €300-500 | €25-60 |
| Email | €10-30 | €150-300 | €10-25 |
| Content Marketing | €15-40 | €200-400 | €15-35 |
| Referral | €5-20 | €100-200 | €5-15 |

### Activation

| Metric | Formula | Description | Target |
|--------|---------|-------------|--------|
| **Activation Rate** | Activated Users / Total Signups × 100% | Users completing key action | 30-50%+ |
| **Time to Value (TTV)** | Days from signup to first value moment | Speed to "aha moment" | < 7 days |
| **Onboarding Completion Rate** | Completed Onboarding / Started × 100% | Onboarding success | > 60% |
| **Feature Adoption Rate** | Users Using Feature / Total Users × 100% | Feature engagement | Varies |
| **Free to Paid Conversion** | Paid Users / Free Users × 100% | Freemium conversion | 2-5% |

**Activation Benchmarks by Product Type:**

| Product Type | Activation Rate | Time to Value |
|--------------|-----------------|---------------|
| SaaS (Simple) | 40-60% | 1-3 days |
| SaaS (Complex) | 20-40% | 7-14 days |
| Consumer App | 30-50% | < 1 day |
| E-commerce | 50-70% (first purchase intent) | Same session |
| Marketplace | 20-40% | 1-7 days |

### Retention

| Metric | Formula | Description | Target |
|--------|---------|-------------|--------|
| **Customer Churn Rate** | Lost Customers / Starting Customers × 100% | Customer loss rate | < 5% monthly |
| **Revenue Churn Rate** | Lost MRR / Starting MRR × 100% | Revenue loss rate | < 3% monthly |
| **Net Revenue Retention (NRR)** | (Start MRR - Churn + Expansion) / Start MRR | Revenue from existing | > 100% |
| **DAU/MAU Ratio** | Daily Active / Monthly Active Users | Stickiness | > 20% |
| **Cohort Retention** | % of cohort active after N periods | Long-term retention | See table |
| **Customer Lifespan** | 1 / Churn Rate | Average customer tenure | > 24 months |

**Cohort Retention Benchmarks:**

| Time | SaaS B2B | Consumer App | E-commerce |
|------|----------|--------------|------------|
| Month 1 | 90-95% | 35-45% | 30-40% |
| Month 3 | 80-90% | 20-30% | 25-35% |
| Month 6 | 75-85% | 15-25% | 20-30% |
| Month 12 | 70-80% | 10-20% | 15-25% |

### Revenue

| Metric | Formula | Description | Target |
|--------|---------|-------------|--------|
| **Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)** | Sum of monthly subscription revenue | Predictable revenue | Track growth |
| **Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR)** | MRR × 12 | Annual subscription | Track growth |
| **Average Revenue Per User (ARPU)** | Total Revenue / Total Users | Revenue per customer | Track growth |
| **Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)** | (ARPU × Gross Margin) / Churn Rate | Total customer value | ≥ 3× CAC |
| **LTV:CAC Ratio** | LTV / CAC | Unit economics health | ≥ 3:1 |
| **Revenue per Employee** | Total Revenue / Employees | Productivity | €100-300K |

**Revenue Metrics Benchmarks:**

| Stage | ARR | MRR Growth | LTV:CAC | CAC Payback |
|-------|-----|------------|---------|-------------|
| Seed | < €1M | > 15% MoM | 2:1 | < 24 mo |
| Series A | €1-5M | 10-15% MoM | 3:1 | < 18 mo |
| Series B | €5-20M | 7-10% MoM | 4:1 | < 15 mo |
| Growth | €20-100M | 5-8% MoM | 5:1 | < 12 mo |
| Scale | > €100M | 3-5% MoM | 5:1+ | < 12 mo |

## Marketing ROI & Attribution

### ROI Calculations

| Metric | Formula |
|--------|---------|
| **Marketing ROI** | (Revenue - Marketing Cost) / Marketing Cost × 100% |
| **ROAS (Return on Ad Spend)** | Revenue from Ads / Ad Spend |
| **Revenue per Channel** | Revenue attributed to channel |
| **Payback Period** | CAC / (Monthly Revenue × Gross Margin) |

**ROI Benchmarks:**

| Channel | Target ROAS | Target ROI |
|---------|-------------|------------|
| Paid Search | 4-8× | 300-700% |
| Paid Social | 3-6× | 200-500% |
| Email | 40-50× | 3900-4900% |
| Content/SEO | 5-10× | 400-900% |
| Affiliate | 6-12× | 500-1100% |

### Attribution Models

| Model | Description | Best For |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| **Last Click** | 100% credit to final touchpoint | Short sales cycles |
| **First Click** | 100% credit to first touchpoint | Brand awareness |
| **Linear** | Equal credit across all touchpoints | Balanced view |
| **Time Decay** | More credit to recent touchpoints | Long sales cycles |
| **Position Based** | 40% first, 40% last, 20% middle | Full-funnel view |
| **Data-Driven** | ML-based attribution | Large data sets |
